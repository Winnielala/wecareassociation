Other human coronaviruses



Delta CoronaVirus

5th of October 2020

Commonly known as Indian severe special infectious pneumonia variant virus, it is one of the known severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus type 2 variants, and humans will suffer from severe special infectious pneumonia after being infected with it.

MERS-CoV

September 2012

Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) is a new respiratory infectious disease caused by Middle East Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus.The human mortality caused by the former is higher than that caused by the latter, but the infectivity is
lower.

SARS-CoV

November 2002 ~ September 2003

Severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS) is an epidemic caused by SARS-CoV. The epidemic spread in 29 countries and regions from November 2002 to September 2003. The primary focus of the epidemic is Shunde City, Guangdong Province, People's Republic of China.
